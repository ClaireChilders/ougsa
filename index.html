The Gender and Sexuality Alliance at Oakland University aims to provide social-, educational-, political-, and entertainment-based programming with an emphasis on community. We host weekly meetings that offer opportunities to interact with queer and allied students. We also hold social gatherings and events throughout the academic year such as bonfires, pride prom, and the annual drag show. We love to connect with other on-campus resources to provide the best experience for all Oakland University students. As the GSA, we aim to build a strong community on campus for all queer and allied students.
